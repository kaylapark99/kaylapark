photography &

some drawings

In seventh grade, I stole my mom's DSLR to pursue my passion for photography. Not going to lie, those photos were terrible. Very terrible. Despite how awful those photos were, my parents saw potential and bought me my own Nikon, and I still use the same camera today. I eventually began to dapple with film photography and sketching.

Whether I am editing in Photoshop, developing in the dark room, or doodling in my sketchbook, I blast music to block out the stress of everyday life. It is just me, my spotify playlists, and my art.

I have divided my art based off of my spotify playlists. Each collection has specific emotions that match the songs of the associated playlist. Simply scan (using the Spotify app) or click on the spotify tag to vibe to some music while perusing my artwork. Enjoy :)

P.S. I have included undeveloped film... maybe one day I will actually devlop them. I used the app FilmBox to invert and scan the negatives, hence the low quality.
